Objectives Of The Trust

  • To provide conducive environment for spreading education in every field of learning, teaching, exchange of knowledge, inculcating good, clean habits,counseling and motivating people into education in all subjects of academic andnon academic vocational and non formal education, including distance education,religious, philosophical, yogic and therapeutic teachings.
  • To sponsor any deserving, needy or exceptional children, differently capacitated,challenged physically, mentally or otherwise, for their education, encouragement and help and to meet the expenditure for the purpose of buying books or meeting their expenses or treating them or preparation for meeting competition or research.
  • Institution of merit and other scholarships to support the good and needy children to pursue or continue education or furthering of their interest.
  • To print publish make hard and soft copies etc. and arrange for sale (nominal value less than or equal to costs and no element of profit) or free distribution of text Books, or other educational materials including story books, General knowledge, career counseling and research books, and learning aids.
  • To create awareness on public in general the need of proper education and value system in the society, conduct competitions and to encourage all in learning and imparting education of every kind.
  • To publish periodicals, journals, newsletters and use modern communication techniques to create awareness, need for education and to update on latest technology, inventions and advancements.
  • To establish and to maintain library, with publication, of suitable literature or journals on all subjects taught at the schools for students including research publications etc.,
  • To establish and organize seminars, conferences and other meetings at regular intervals so as to create learning atmosphere and to promote the objective of education.
  • To get affiliations, certifications and other registrations as may be required from time to time to effectively pursue any of the objects said above.
  • To create an environment of continuous learning for all involved in execution or pursuance of the above objects.
  • To co-ordinate, act jointly or otherwise with other institutions in furtherance with the objects similar to those of the Trust.
  • To take over, manage, fund and run other institutions with similar objectives.
  • To run the Trust only for the purpose of Charity in nature without any element of profit motive.
  • To do all such lawful things those are incidental, ancillary or conductive to the attainment of the above objectives.
  • To do all acts to get affiliation, registrations and other recognition of Educational and other Boards, Universities and other Government and non Government organization in furtherance of any objectives of the trust.
  • The trust shall not carry on or current any of the activities outside India.
  • To receive or collect funds by subscriptions, donations or any other means for the objectives and running of the trust.

All the above objectives are oriented towards the society at large irrespective ofc aste, creed, sex, religion, color or any boundaries.The income and funds will be solely utilized towards the objects and no portion of it will be utilized for payment to Trustees by way of profit dividends, interest etc.

The Trust shall be a Public Charitable Trust and a non profit organization and the income accruing to the Trust shall be used only and totally for furthering the objects of the Trust and no part of such income shall be shared among the Trustees or distributed to any one or more of the Trustees.
